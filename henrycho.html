About Me

History

I grew up in Los Angeles, California, where I attended Rosemead High School. During my time in high school, I was interested in the sciences, but it wasn't until my senior year when I decided to join the Science Olympiad team. For me, applying science to engineer efficient devices was much different than solving textbook problems. I have to face the problems of real world effects, where in textbook problems usually assume negligible. Even though I did not have much experience in applying science, I still considered to pursue an engineering degree. I applyed and was accepted to the Uniersity of Michigan's College of Engineering.

University of Michigan

I was accepted to University of Michigan's College of Engineering undergraduate program, but I still wasn't entirely sure of in which field of engineering I should specialize. Previously, I had decided on aerospace engineering when my father took me to NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory, but a number of pressing environmental issues has risen since my childhood, so I questioned on whether I should pursue a degree in civil and environmental engineering. I decided when I found out I could still apply my aerospace knowledge to environmental efforts and continues to pursue my childhood dream.
